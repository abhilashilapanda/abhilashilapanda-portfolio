Abhilash is a Senior Consultant at Axtria with over 4 years of analytical and consulting experience in the US pharmaceutical industry. He specialises in long-range forecasting and commercial analytics — primarily for oncology portfolios, with deep expertise in breast cancer.

He builds patient-based and revenue forecast models integrating epidemiology, patient flow, competitor share, launch dynamics, and policy impacts such as IRA and loss of exclusivity — enabling strategic planning for both pipeline and marketed assets.

His work spans multi-scenario market-sizing workbooks, secondary research using Evaluate Pharma and MIDAS datasets, VBA-powered pricing simulation tools, and interactive dashboards that drive data-informed decisions across US and international markets.

Beyond analytics, Abhilash is actively exploring Agentic AI — studying how autonomous AI systems are reshaping the future of data work and business operations.
